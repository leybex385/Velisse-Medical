Meet the Founder

Maya Miller

Senior Partner, Velisse Medical

Maya Miller is the Senior Partner of Velisse Medical, an international medical aesthetics equipment trading company specializing in global sourcing, healthcare technology distribution, and cross-border medical solutions.

She has over 16 years of experience in international business, healthcare trade, and global market development, with extensive expertise in North American and Asia-Pacific markets.

Throughout her career, Maya has successfully built strategic partnerships between manufacturers, distributors, hospitals, and aesthetic clinics, helping healthcare organizations access advanced medical technologies through efficient international supply chains.

Today, Maya leads the company's international strategy, helping healthcare providers and aesthetic clinics gain access to innovative medical technologies through compliant global sourcing and distribution networks.

Philanthropy

Healthcare Without Borders Initiative

In 2022, Maya led Velisse Medical's "Healthcare Without Borders Initiative," partnering with nonprofit organizations to donate patient monitoring devices and essential medical equipment to community clinics in Southeast Asia.

The initiative supplied digital blood pressure monitors, pulse oximeters, and portable oxygen concentrators to underserved healthcare facilities while also supporting clinical staff through equipment training and technical guidance.

To date, the program has benefited more than 2,000 patients by improving access to basic diagnostic technologies.
